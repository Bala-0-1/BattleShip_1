How To Play?

  • On the opponent side of the sea, boats will be hidden. Your goal is to destroy all of them before your opponent destroys yours.
  • Choose a square where you think a boat might be present. If it is, congratulations. You have hit a part of a boat.
  • Boats are laid out in either horizontal or vertical direction.
  • If you destroy all of your opponent's boats, You win the game.
